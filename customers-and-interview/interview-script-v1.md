# Interview Script v1

**Team Name:** localhost:3000  
**Problem this script is for:** Backend and frontend developers have trouble communicating API details during development, causing delays and repeated clarification  
**ICP this script targets:** Junior–mid developers (students and working developers) who collaborate on API-based projects  
**Version:** v1.0  
**Date created:** 22 March 2026

---

## Screener Questions

Before scheduling the interview, confirm:

1. "Are you currently working on a project where you interact with APIs (either building or using them)?"
2. "Do you usually work with at least one other developer (frontend or backend)?"
3. "Have you worked on a team project in the last 2–3 months?"

---

## Interview Introduction

"Thanks for taking the time — I really appreciate it. We’re doing research for a university project where we’re trying to understand how developers collaborate when building applications, especially around backend and frontend work.

We’re not building anything yet and not pitching anything — we just want to understand your experience and how things actually work for you.

There are no right or wrong answers.

Is it okay if I take notes while we talk?"

---

## Phase 1: Opening and Context (5 minutes)

**Question 1:**  
"Can you walk me through what a typical development workflow looks like for you when you’re working on a project?"

**Question 2:**  
"What kind of projects are you usually working on, and what is your role in them?"

**Follow-up prompts:**

- "Can you tell me more about that?"
- "How does your team usually collaborate?"

---

## Phase 2: Problem Exploration (10 minutes)

**Question 3:**  
"What parts of working in a team on a project do you find most frustrating or time-consuming?"

**Question 4:**  
"Is there anything in your development workflow that feels repetitive or like unnecessary effort?"

**Question 5:**  
"Can you tell me about a recent situation where something in your development process didn’t go as planned?"

---

## Phase 3: Problem Deep Dive (10 minutes)

_(If API-related communication comes up naturally, go deeper. If not, introduce gently.)_

"One area we’ve been curious about is how developers communicate API details between backend and frontend — has that ever come up for you?"

**Question 6:**  
"Can you walk me through the last time you had to work with an API created by someone else (or that someone else had to use)? What happened step by step?"

**Question 7:**  
"You mentioned [their example]. Why did you handle it that way?"

**Question 8:**  
"When that process doesn’t go smoothly, what usually happens? What does it cost you in terms of time or effort?"

---

## Phase 4: Current Solutions (5 minutes)

**Question 9:**  
"What do you currently use to share or understand API details in your team?"

**Question 10:**  
"How well does that work for you? What parts of it are frustrating or missing?"

---

## Phase 5: Close (5 minutes)

**Question 11 (catch-all):**  
"Is there anything about working with APIs or collaborating with other developers that I haven’t asked about but that you think is important?"

**Referral question:**  
"Do you know anyone else who works in a similar way that might be worth talking to?"

**Close:**  
"Thanks — this was really helpful. If something comes up later, would it be okay if we follow up with a quick message?"
